What Is LAYN?

LAYN is an IT community.
Its name comes from the written pronunciation of the word “line”.

It represents the idea of combining
technology and discipline on a single line.

The line is not about perfection.
It is about direction.

| — Administrators.
They stand at the center of the line and keep balance.

/ — The left arm.
Software development and technical operations.

\ — The right arm.
Security, defense, and protection.

- — Members.
The foundation that forms the line itself.

The symbol of LAYN is ~.
It represents ups, downs, and constant change.
